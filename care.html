MAKE IT LAST

Flower care

A few simple steps to keep your FLOWERBX stems at their best for as long as possible.

01

Trim the stems

Cut 2-3cm off each stem at an angle before placing in water, and re-cut every few days.

02

Change the water

Refresh the water every two days and use the flower food provided.

03

Keep them cool

Avoid direct sun, radiators and draughts — cooler rooms make blooms last longer.

04

Remove foliage

Strip any leaves that would sit below the waterline to keep the water clean.

05

Deadhead often

Remove spent blooms so the arrangement keeps its shape and energy.

06

Mind the vase

A clean vase, free of residue, helps prevent bacteria that shorten vase life.

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, most FLOWERBX arrangements stay beautiful for 7-10 days.

Do all flowers need the same care?

Most tips apply broadly, though delicate blooms like peonies prefer cooler conditions and less handling.
